    Notation tips
    • SAN/PGN: e4, Nf3, O-O, Qxd5+, c8=Q#.
    • Standard numbering pairs both sides on one turn: 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6.
    • Also accepted: numbered half-moves (1. e4 2. e5 3. Nf3), repeated turn numbers (1. e4 1. e5), or Black dots (1... e5).
    • Legacy coordinates: Pf2f4, Nb8c6.
    • Captures show beside the board with a running material score.
    • Notation pause clock: typing pauses the timer; typing a move number (1., 2., 1...) presses the clock for the side to move. Edit freely until then.
    • Live clock: follows each completed move and keeps running while you type.
    • Flip the board for couch co-op without turning the device.
    • CPU player (off by default): toss a coin for White, then enter your moves in notation — Stockfish replies automatically. Strength uses Stockfish skill / Elo limits from beginner through full engine. Pace (on by default) makes the CPU take about your average think time so the clocks stay even; turn it off for instant replies at the chosen strength.

    acnab is free on GitHub Pages. Source on GitHub. Optional CPU opponent uses Stockfish.js (GPLv3).
